A full stack developer portfolio is a collection of work samples that demonstrates a full stack developer's skills, knowledge, and experience. It can be used to apply for jobs, attract new clients, or showcase one's work to the public. A full stack developer is someone who can build and maintain both the front-end and back-end of a web application. This means that they have skills in both front-end development technologies (such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript) and back-end development technologies (such as Python, Java, or Ruby on Rails).
